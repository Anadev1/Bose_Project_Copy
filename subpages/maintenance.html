Cleaning your earbuds

Taking care of your headphones can benefit your financial state, while also protecting your health. Cleaning your headphones regularly improves sound quality and prolongs their lifespan so you donâ€™t have to buy a new pair of headphones every year. It also prevents you from catching any bacteria or infection that may damage your ear.
All you need in order to clean your headphones/earbuds:
  • A cloth
  • Soft toothbrush
  • Water and soap
  • Q-tip
  • Sanitizer or alcohol

Cleaning your on-ear headphones

Cleaning the exterior of the headphones can be done using some cloth dampened in soap water, after removing the earpads. Then, it can be dried with paper towels and left to air dry so that all the moisture is vanished.
In order to clean the ear pads and head cushions, you should first wipe them down with some warm soap water, let them dry completely after wiping with some paper towels and then start wiping them with an alcohol dampened cloth. For a complete cleaning, it is also recommended to use q-tips in order to reach all the crevices and take the dirt out completely.
